Welcome to Education City

Education City in Geedam, Dantewada, is a prominent educational hub designed
to enhance learning opportunities in the region.
It hosts a variety of institutions,
from primary schools to higher education facilities,catering to diverse educational needs.
The city is committed to fostering academic excellence and personal development,
with well-equipped classrooms, modern teaching methods, and extracurricular activities.
Education City also emphasizes community involvement and strives to create a supportive
environment for both students and educators..
